Famous Foods of Kerala

Appam

A soft, lacy rice pancake usually served with stew or coconut milk.

Puttu

A cylindrical steamed rice cake with coconut, typically eaten with kadala curry.

Kerala Sadya

A grand vegetarian feast served on a banana leaf, featuring multiple dishes.

Kerala Fish Curry

A spicy and tangy fish curry made with coconut and tamarind.

Banana Fritters

Also called "Pazham Pori", these are sweet banana slices deep-fried in batter.
