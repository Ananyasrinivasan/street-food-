Famous Foods of Karnataka

Bisi Bele Bath

A spicy rice and lentil dish cooked with tamarind, vegetables, and special masala.

Mysore Masala Dosa

A crispy dosa with a spicy red chutney inside, served with sambar and coconut chutney.

Ragi Mudde

Traditional finger millet balls, eaten with spicy sambar or curry.

Mangalorean Biryani

Fragrant biryani with coastal flavors, made with coconut-based masala.

Mysore Pak

A rich and buttery sweet made with gram flour, ghee, and sugar.
