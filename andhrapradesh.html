Famous Foods of Andhra Pradesh

Pesarattu

A crispy green gram dosa, often served with ginger chutney.

Gongura Pachadi

A spicy and tangy chutney made with gongura leaves, a signature Andhra dish.

Andhra Biryani

Spicy and flavorful biryani, known for its strong masala and unique taste.

Pootharekulu

A traditional Andhra sweet made of thin rice paper, sugar, and ghee.

Ulavacharu

A delicious horse gram soup, often enjoyed with rice and ghee.
